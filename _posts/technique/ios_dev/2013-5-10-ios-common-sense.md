## How to find your device's UDID ?

`UDID` is the identifier of your device, it is unique.

check it by connect your device to itunes or iTools or Xcode(window->devices)


##  iOS 模拟器app的Document目录在哪里？
参考下面这个路径：
/Users/<your-user-name>/Library/Developer/CoreSimulator/Devices/<a-hex-string>/data/Containers/Data/Application/<a-hex-string>/Documents/
